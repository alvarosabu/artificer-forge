Enemies are hostile actors that belong to the `hostile` team. They are driven by aggressive AI, have defined alert and attack ranges, and can drop loot on defeat.

## Characteristics


| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| `type` | `character` |
| `subtype` | `enemy` |
| `team` | `hostile` |
| `controllable` | `false` |
| Component | `Actor.vue` |
| AI | `aggressive` — pursues and attacks on sight |

## Visual Distinctions

:::div{class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 items-start"}

- **Nameplate**: Red text + skull icon <UIcon name="ph:skull-fill" class="size-4 text-white drop-shadow-[0_0_2px_#dc2626]" />; subtitle shows `Lv. X · Race` when defined
- **Outline on hover**: Red (hostile color)
- **HP bar**: Visible on hover, red progress bar

## Stats

Enemies have lighter stat blocks focused on offense and threshold behavior:

```yaml
stats:
  hp: 12
  maxHp: 12
  ac: 13
  strength: 8
  dexterity: 14
```

## AI

Aggressive AI defines spatial awareness and combat thresholds:

```yaml
ai:
  behavior: aggressive
  alertRange: 10    # Distance at which enemy notices the player
  attackRange: 5    # Distance at which enemy initiates attack
  fleeThreshold: 0.2  # HP % at which enemy flees
```

## Drops

Enemies can drop items on defeat with configurable quantity ranges and chance:

```yaml
drops:
  - id: gold
    quantity: [1, 5]
    chance: 0.8
  - id: dagger_rusty
    chance: 0.2
```
