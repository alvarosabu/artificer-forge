title: Player Characters

---

Player characters are controlled directly by the user via pointer (click-to-move) or keyboard input. They belong to the `player` team and are always members of the party.

## Characteristics

:::div{class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 items-start"}

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| `type` | `character` |
| `team` | `player` |
| `controllable` | `true` |
| Component | `Character.vue` |
| Movement | `useCharacterController` (pointer/keyboard) |

:::

## Visual Distinctions

- **Nameplate**: White text, no icon; subtitle shows `Lv. X · Race` when defined
- **Outline on hover**: None — the active playable character has no outline
- **Target indicator**: Appears at move destination when clicking the floor

## Stats

Player characters carry full D&D-style stats plus action economy fields:

```yaml
stats:
  hp: 38
  maxHp: 38
  ac: 14
  strength: 12
  dexterity: 16
  constitution: 12
  intelligence: 10
  wisdom: 14
  charisma: 10
  maxActions: 1
  maxBonusActions: 1
```

## Equipment

Weapons are attached to hand bones via `useEquipment`. The `mainHand` and `offHand` slots reference weapon `templateId` values:

```yaml
equipment:
  mainHand: shortsword
```
