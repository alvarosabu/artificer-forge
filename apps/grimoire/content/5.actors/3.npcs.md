NPCs are world inhabitants that are not controlled by the player and pose no threat. They belong to the `neutral` team and are rendered via `Actor.vue`.

## Characteristics

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| `type` | `character` |
| `subtype` | `npc` |
| `team` | `neutral` |
| `controllable` | `false` |
| Component | `Actor.vue` |
| AI | `idle` — loops a defined animation |


## Visual Distinctions

:::div{class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-6 items-start"}

- **Nameplate**: White text, no icon; subtitle shows `Lv. X · Race` when defined
- **Outline on hover**: White (neutral color)
- **No target indicator** — not selectable for movement

![Neutral Actor](/neutral-actor.png)

:::



## Stats

NPCs carry full combat stats even if not engaged in combat, reflecting their role and rank in the world:

```yaml
stats:
  hp: 45
  maxHp: 45
  ac: 18
  strength: 16
  dexterity: 12
  constitution: 14
  intelligence: 10
  wisdom: 12
  charisma: 14
```

## AI

Idle NPCs loop a single animation defined in the YAML:

```yaml
ai:
  behavior: idle
  idleAnimation: Idle_B
```
