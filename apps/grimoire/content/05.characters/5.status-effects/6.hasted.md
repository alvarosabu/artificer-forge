A speed buff that displays a blue lightning bolt badge. Currently badge-only with no 3D shader overlay or particles.

## Badge

::status-effect-badge{effect-id="hasted"}
::

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Icon | `i-lucide-zap` |
| Color | `text-blue-400` |
| Background | `bg-blue-900` |

## Shader Overlay

No shader overlay is applied for hasted.

## Animation Reaction

No animation reaction is triggered for hasted.

## Particles

No particles are spawned for hasted.
