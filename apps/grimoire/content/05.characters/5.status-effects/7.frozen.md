## Shader Overlay

<!-- TODO: Add screenshot of frozen overlay on character -->

The frozen overlay uses a hex lattice pattern generated from three cosine waves to create a crystalline ice effect:

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Noise type | Hex lattice (3 cosine waves at ~60° angles) |
| Interior color | `#1144aa` (deep blue) |
| Edge color | `#aaddff` (cyan) |
| Pulse speed | `0.4` |

### Frost Spread

The effect uses a height mask that makes frost spread **upward from the ground**. Full coverage at the feet, gradually fading above mid-torso. This creates the impression of ice creeping up from below.

### How It Works

`buildFrozenEmissiveNode()` generates the hex lattice in TSL by combining three cosine waves offset at approximately 60° angles. The resulting pattern creates sharp hexagonal cells. The interior and edge colors are interpolated based on the lattice value, producing a faceted crystalline look similar to BG3's frozen effect. A subtle time-based pulse adds a gentle shimmer.
