---
title: Damage Numbers
description: Floating damage and healing numbers anchored to characters in 3D space
---

Damage numbers moved out of the character system and into the dedicated VFX package, `@artificer-forge/vfx`. The `Character` component still consumes them internally (it runs `useDamageNumbers` and exposes `showDamage`), but the composable, the `DamageNumber` component, and the full API now live alongside the other visual effects.

::alert{type="info"}
See [Damage Numbers](/vfx/damage-numbers) in the VFX section for the full API, colors, CSS animation, and integration details.
::
